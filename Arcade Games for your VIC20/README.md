## DAZZLE YOURSELF WITH TWENTY STUNNING GAMES FOR YOUR VIC 20

If you are ready to realize your VIC20's potential
as a powerful arcade games machine,
read on - this book is for you.

Whizz kid Brett Hale has put together an outstanding
collection of twenty complete arcade games,
each of them perfect for the unexpanded VIC 20.
All the programs are listed twice - once for 
straightforward kyeboard play, and once for
using with a joytick.  All the games have been
extensively play-tested, and have passed with flying colours.
