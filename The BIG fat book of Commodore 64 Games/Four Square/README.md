This is an unusual board game, in which you have to try and possess as many squares in a group of four as you can.  Thould be clear when you look at the board at the start of the game:

The object of the game is to gather your markers (H) in a square.  This is not connect four, in a line does not count.

   1  2  3  4  5  6  7/n
A  .  .  .  .  .  .  ./n
B  .  .  .  .  .  .  C/n
C  .  H  H  .  .  .  ./n
D  .  H  H  C  .  .  ./n
E  .  .  .  .  .  .  ./n
F  .  .  C  .  .  .  ./n
G  .  .  .  .  .  .  ./n

The above scenario is a win to the player.  
Player is marked with a H, and the computer is marked with a C.
