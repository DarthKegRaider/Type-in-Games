## SUB ATTACK

Once again you're under pressure.  There are nine submarines in teh vicinity of your destroyer.  You have to try and plot their co-ordinates, and drop your depth charges.  Hits and misses will be shown on a graph.

When playing with a joystick, moving the joystick to the right will increase the co-ordinate number, while moving to the left will decrease the number.  Your fire button releases the depth charges.

** This game originally came as two separate type in games with different control types.  I've modified the final version "sub attack" as a combined version which you select at the beginning of the game which method you wish to use.  Of course, I have kept the original keyboard and joystick varients on the D64 image should you wish to inspect the original code also.
