Your mission, brave defender of battered Terra, is once again to destroy the pesky invaders.

You move your laser cannon left and right, attempting to avoid the invaders' bombs.  When you're in the clear, you can fire your laser, and attempt to shoot a few invaders down.

In the keyboard version, "J" moves you left, "K" moves you right and the space bar fires your cannon.

** Created an "Enhanced" version which combines the Joystick and Keyboard controls, plus created an intro screen with a Title, original author and brief instructions.
