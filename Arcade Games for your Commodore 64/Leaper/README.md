## Leaper

Time to try and hop across the road, avoiding the
oncoming cars. Once you've made it safely across,
you have to try and jump onto the moving logs.
Be careful not to jump onto the back of a log, or
you'll be stranded. Once you've reached the last
log, you have to try and jump into the home base, to
score even more points.
Here are your control keys:
"W" moves you up
"A" moves you left
"D" moves you right
"S" moves you down

**Game file has both the keyboard and joystick control methods selectable at the new title page.**
