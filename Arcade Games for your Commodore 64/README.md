EXPLORE THE POWER AND POTENTIAL OF YOUR COMMODORE 64 WITH THESE STUNNING ARCADE GAMES.
The Commodore 64, with its massive memory, superb sound and sophisticate graphics, is the perfect micro for you to use for arcade games - especially when the games have been put together by whizz kid Brett Hale.

All the games have been extensively play-tested and work brilliantly as they are; but because they're all in BASIC you can modify them as you wish.  What's more, each game is listed twice - once for keyboard, and once for use with a joystick.

Games included are:
* Tick
* City Terror
* Brick Layer
* Surface Lander
and others.
