Terrorists have bombed the airport runway in your city.  Because of this, you're stranded up in the air - unable to land - in a plane carrying a full load of ammunition.  Your only hope is to try and clear the runway with your bombs, and make a perfect three-point landing.

Just press any key to drop a bomb in the keyboard version, and use the fire key for bomb dropping in the joystick version.

**  I've combined both versions in the D64 image, and have not created them separate as yet.
